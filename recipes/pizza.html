Pizza

  1. 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  2. 1 cup pizza sauce
  3. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  6. 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  7. 1/4 cup sliced onions
  8. 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  9. 1/4 cup crumbled Italian sausage
  10. 1/4 cup crumbled cooked bacon
  11. Dried oregano
  12. Dried basil
  13. Garlic powder
  14. Olive oil

    15. Instructions: Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended on the pizza dough package. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Add toppings: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, sausage, and bacon. Season: Sprinkle with dried oregano, basil, and garlic powder. Drizzle with olive oil. Bake: Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and serve: Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
